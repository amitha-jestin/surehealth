ProductName - SureHealth

## Users
Parents and patients seeking affordable medical second opinions.

## Problem
Patients need a reliable, accessible way to confirm medical treatments, ensuring safety and necessity.

## Solution
A web-based platform to submit medical cases and receive asynchronous written opinions from verified specialists worldwide.

## Target Users
Patients recovering from surgeries or critical treatments.
Families seeking clarity and trust in medical decisions.
Patients in regions with limited access to specialists.
Users who prefer remote consultations to avoid repeated hospital visits.

## Business Goals
Provide affordable, accessible second-opinion service globally.
Build trust and credibility among patients and doctors.

## Scope
Second opinion and guidance only. No diagnosis, prescriptions, or emergency care.
